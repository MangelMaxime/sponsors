Welcome to my sponsors & backers page.

I am Maxime Mangel, a software engineer passionate about open source and F#.

For more than seven years, I have been an open source maintainer for Fable and the F# ecosystem.

I am a lead developer of Fable project, a F# to JavaScript compiler.

In parallel, I maintain libraries to make the F# ecosystem better.

  • Thoth.Json: makes your JSON type safe and generates human friendly errors in case of errors
  • Fable.Form: makes working with form in Elmish easy and scalable
  • Elmish HMR: make Elmish application support Hot Reload allowing you to make modification on the fly to your application
  • If you want to see the full list of my projects, you can go to my:
    • Github profile
    • NuGet profile
    • NPM profile

Finally, because I am passionate about UX/DX, I often contribute to the F# tooling, with projects like Ionide and FsAutoComplete.

My major contribution to these projects are in regard to tooltip formatting and syntax highlighting
